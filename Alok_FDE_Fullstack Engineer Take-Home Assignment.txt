Assignment
The Problem
Law enforcement needs to redact sensitive information from long PDF files before public
release. Currently, the process is entirely manual: Ctrl+F to find names, addresses, SSNs, then
manually draw black boxes over each instance. For a 100-page document, this takes hours and
it's easy to miss things.
We can use AI to automatically detect and suggest redactions, but the UX is the hard part.
How do you design an interface that's actually faster than the current workflow while ensuring
nothing gets missed?
